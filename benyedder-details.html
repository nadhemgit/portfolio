In today’s fast-paced, ever-changing business world, it’s more important than ever to stand out from the crowd and give your brand a unique identity. We live in a visual world where first impressions are made almost immediately. This means that your business needs to be designed in a way that makes it stand out as something memorable and engaging.

A coffee cart is one of the most commonly used work stations for baristas, and they’re also arguably some of the cutest. We had a good time designing the cart to achieve that level of modernity and delicacy.
