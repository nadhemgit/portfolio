Small Appartment

Project information

  • Category: Interior
  • Client: Mrs. J
  • Project date: December, 2018

This was a simple yet fun project to create. It's a simple design that has all the basics of a modern and compact appartment. We had to work with a small space and make most of it by combining the kitchen and the living room. We also took advantage of the natural light by creating wide openings and glass seperations.
